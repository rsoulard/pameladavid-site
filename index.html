Running for Macomb County Board of Commissioners

In District 8, Clinton Township and Mount Clemens

Working For The People!

Why I Am Running

I am running for County Commissioner to be your voice, the one that is heard, and the one who will address your concerns and act upon them.

I will hold the County responsible, always keeping in mind, I work for you, the Residents of Clinton Township.

I will do what is in the best interest of Clinton Township and Mount Clemens, Residents and Businesses alike, to ensure your health, safety, welfare and investments are protected.

My Mission Statement

My vision for Macomb County includes strengthening public safety, fixing our crumbling infrastructure and enhancing services for our seniors and veterans.

My Values

I am the person:

  • You can trust and who will listen and hear your voice.
  • Who will facilitate change while being mindful of what your needs are.
  • Who will bring reason and logic to the table.
  • Who has the ability to compromise and reach across the table without bias.
  • Who works hard and gives her heart to her community in an effort to give back unconditionally.

My loyalty, commitment and dedication to work tirelessly for my constituents will aid in moving Clinton Township and Mount Clemens through these difficult times so that we will remain a strong and prosperous community of Macomb County.

Alone we can do so little; Together we can do so much.

Helen Keller
